Style Transfer

Evan Hernandez and David Liang

A TensorFlow CNN Implementation



Summary

We used a pre-trained 19-layer convolutional neural network to implement the method proposed by Gatys et al to transfer the style of one image onto the content of another. We then extended the project to allow for video style transfer, as described by Ruder et al.
